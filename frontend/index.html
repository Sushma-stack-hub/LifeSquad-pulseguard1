Health Tips 💡

Simple things you can do every day to keep your heart happy.

🥗

Eat Less Salt

Try to keep salt under 5g per day. Avoid processed foods like chips and instant noodles. Home cooked meals are your friend!

🚶

Walk 30 Minutes Daily

Even a gentle 30-minute walk makes a huge difference for blood pressure. You don't need to run — just keep moving.

😴

Sleep 7–8 Hours

Poor sleep can raise your blood pressure. Try to go to bed and wake up at the same time each day.

🫁

Breathe Deeply

5 minutes of slow deep breathing lowers your BP on the spot. Breathe in 4 counts, hold 4, out for 6.

☕

Limit Caffeine

Too much coffee or tea can spike blood pressure. Keep it to 1–2 cups a day and avoid it in the evenings.

💊

Take Meds as Prescribed

If your doctor prescribed medication, take it every day even when you feel fine. Missing doses is one of the most common reasons BP stays high.
